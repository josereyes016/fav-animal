Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky dominated native breeds in the 400 mile ‘All-Alaska Sweepstakes’ race from Nome to Candle in 1910.

  • Dog Breed Group: Working Dogs
  • Height: 1' 8'' to 1' 11''
  • Weight: 30 to 60 lb
  • Lifespan: 12 to 15 years

Huskys are active and playfull dogs. They don't like to be alone so it best to have two. It is common for them to have two different color eyes and usually have blue, green, hazel, brown eyes.
